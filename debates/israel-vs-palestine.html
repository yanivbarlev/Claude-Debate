Why the Crowd Landed at 35

The crowd leaned toward the Pro-Palestine position primarily because of the weight of international legal consensus — the ICJ's near-unanimous 2004 advisory opinion, the 2024 opinion declaring the occupation illegal, UNSC Resolution 2334 on settlements, and the Geneva Convention's Article 49.
